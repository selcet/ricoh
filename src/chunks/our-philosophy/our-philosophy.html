Our Philosophy
in code we trust
We strongly believe that communication and accurate information are very important. No matter if there are good or bad news - you're up-to-date with everything. You can always call us or talk to us via dedicated Slack room which will be created for your project.

As we work in agile methodologies, our development process is very simple and well defined - Backlog Refinements, Sprint Review, Planning, regular meetings.

You have full access to both JIRA (Project Management system) and GIT Repos all the time. You can measure our progress on a daily basis - we want to be 100% clear with all we do.
